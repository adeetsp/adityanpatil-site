I've noticed something about myself: when I keep my fitness private, I negotiate with
it. When people can see it, I show up.

So this bucket is my accountability system. Training blocks, how my body's feeling,
what I'm eating (and the weeks where "what I'm eating" is a crime scene — RIP my diet
more often than I'd like to admit). Numbers included, because I love a concrete stat
and "I've been working out" means nothing without one.

The rule for this section is honesty over highlight reel. If I'm in a slump, that
gets written down too. Honestly, those posts will probably be the more useful ones —
anyone can write about a good week.

If you're into fitness, follow along and call me out when the posts stop showing up.
That's kind of the point.
