Somewhere along the way I started mentally sorting my life into four buckets:
**Personal**, **Physical**, **Professional**, and **Philosophical**. It started as a
way to check in on myself — like, which of these is getting my energy right now, and
which one is quietly starving?

The nice thing about the framework is that it's honest. On any given month, one
bucket is thriving and another one is a mess, and seeing them side by side keeps me
from pretending otherwise. Great quarter at work but haven't called my friends in
three weeks? The buckets know.

This site is literally that framework, published. Four squares on the homepage, one
for each P. My hope is that if you read across all four, you don't just get my
updates — you get *me*. How I think, what I care about, what I'm working on becoming.

The Philosophical bucket specifically is where the mindset stuff lives. Not abstract
theory — I'm not going to lecture you about Stoicism — more like specific moments
that changed how I think about something, written down while they're fresh.

That's the whole pitch. Pick a square and poke around.
