If you know me, you know I'm long overdue for putting life updates somewhere that
isn't a group chat.

This is that place. The Personal bucket is where I'll drop what's actually going on —
the moves, the trips, the people, and yes, a probably unreasonable amount of updates
about my dog. If we haven't caught up in a while and you're wondering what I've been
up to, this section will do a better job than my texting habits ever will.

Fair warning: this is the least polished corner of the site, on purpose. Think of it
less like a blog and more like the update I'd give you if we grabbed dinner.

More soon. I've got a backlog of stories and honestly, writing them down is half the
reason this site exists.
